Setting up the SAP Smart Business Modeler Apps Framework

The following information is relevant when setting up the SAP Smart Business modeler apps framework.

Installation of SAP Smart Business Modeler Apps on the Front-End Server

On the front-end server, you must install the SAP Smart Business modeler apps product. The SAP Smart Business modeler apps product is part of the User Interface Add-On 1.0 for SAP NetWeaver.

Installation of SAP Smart Business Products on SAP HANA Server

According to the SAP Fiori app that you want to use, you must install the respective SAP Smart Business product version on your SAP HANA server. After installation, the software components included in the product version are available on your SAP HANA server.
